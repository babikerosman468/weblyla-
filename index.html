Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Lula Support

Our Story

Lula Support was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to empower communities through technology and collaboration. We believe that when people come together and share resources, incredible things can happen.

Our platform provides tools and resources that help communities organize, share knowledge, and support one another. From local neighborhood groups to global open-source projects, Lula Support is here to help you make a difference.
